Our Story

Founded in 2008, Famous Pest Control Services began with a vision to revolutionize pest control in India. Our founders, experienced pest control professionals, recognized the need for safe, effective, and eco-friendly solutions that protect both people and the environment.

Over 15 years, we've grown from a small local business to India's most trusted pest control company, serving over 50,000 satisfied customers across India. Our commitment to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction has made us the preferred choice for residential, commercial, and industrial pest management.
