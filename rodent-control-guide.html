Complete Guide to Rodent Control and Prevention

Learn professional methods to eliminate mice and rats from your home safely and effectively. Protect your family from diseases and property damage.

Understanding Rodent Problems

Rodents like mice and rats are not just nuisances; they pose serious health risks and can cause significant property damage. In India, common rodent species include house mice, brown rats, and black rats. These pests can carry diseases, contaminate food, and damage electrical wiring and structures.

Signs of Rodent Infestation

Early detection is crucial for effective rodent control. Look for these common signs:

  • Droppings: Small, dark pellets near food sources
  • Gnaw marks: Teeth marks on wood, plastic, or wires
  • Nesting materials: Shredded paper, fabric, or insulation
  • Scratching sounds: Noises in walls or ceilings at night
  • Grease marks: Dark smudges along walls and baseboards

Professional Rodent Control Methods

Our certified technicians use a comprehensive approach to eliminate rodents:

1. Inspection and Assessment

We conduct a thorough inspection to identify entry points, nesting areas, and the extent of the infestation. This helps us develop a targeted treatment plan.

2. Exclusion Techniques

We seal all entry points using materials that rodents cannot chew through, preventing future infestations.

3. Trapping and Removal

We use humane trapping methods to capture and remove existing rodents from your property.

4. Sanitation and Cleanup

We clean and sanitize affected areas to remove odors and prevent attracting new rodents.

Prevention Tips

Prevent future rodent problems with these simple steps:

  • Keep food in sealed containers
  • Remove clutter that provides hiding places
  • Seal cracks and gaps in walls and foundations
  • Keep garbage in tightly sealed containers
  • Trim vegetation away from your home

  • Safe Methods: Professional-grade products and techniques
  • Complete Elimination: Addresses the root cause of the problem
  • Prevention Focus: Long-term solutions to prevent reinfestation
